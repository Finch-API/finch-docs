# Employer

Employer products are specified by the `product` parameter, a space-separated list of products that your application is requesting access to during Finch Connect. Valid product names areâ€”
* `company`: Read basic company data
* `directory`:  Read company directory and organization structure
* `individual`: Read employee data, excluding income and employment data
* `employment`: Read employee employment and income data
* `payment`: Read payroll and contractor related payments by the company
* `pay-statement`: Read detailed pay-statements for each employee
